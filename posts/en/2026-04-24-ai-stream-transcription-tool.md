## Practical Tips to Get the Most Out of AI Transcription

A few field-tested best practices to maximize transcription quality:

**Start with great audio.** AI transcription is powerful, but it's only as good as the input signal. A quality microphone, a well-treated room, and a consistent volume level always make a difference.

**Speak clearly during key moments.** For important announcements or segments you know you'll want to repurpose, slow down slightly and enunciate. The transcription will be more accurate and need fewer corrections.

**Build a custom glossary.** Some tools let you add specific terms — usernames, game titles, community catchphrases — to boost recognition accuracy. Spend ten minutes setting this up: the improvement in precision is immediate.

**Always proofread before publishing.** No matter how capable the AI is, a quick read-through is still essential before posting an article or locking in final subtitles.
